Welcome to UiTM Student e-Profile!

The main purpose of the platform is to serve as an online portal for students of Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM). The e-Profile system is likely to offer a centralized and easy platform for students to manage various aspects of their academic and administrative life within the university. One of the core functions of such a website is to provide students with access to their personal academic records. Essentially, the UiTM Student e-Profile website aims to enhance the student experience by providing a user-friendly digital interface for self-service. It is likely to reduce the administrative burden on both students and university staff by automating routine queries and providing easily accessible information, thus contributing to a more efficient and accessible educational environment at UiTM.

OUR VISION

To empower every student with a dynamic, comprehensive, and easily accessible digital portfolio that showcases their academic achievements, extracurricular engagements, skill development, and personal growth, fostering self-awareness, future readiness, and lifelong learning.


OUR MISSION

To provide a centralized, dynamic, and comprehensive digital record of a student's academic journey, achievements, and developmental progress.
